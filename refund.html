Refund Policy

When and how you can get a refund for Pro or Lifetime.

Last updated: July 6, 2026
This page is a starting-point template, not legal advice. Have a qualified lawyer review it before publishing.

Overview

We want you to feel confident upgrading to Pro or Lifetime. If IVD isn't working out, we offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on both plans, no complicated hoops required.

Eligibility

You're eligible for a full refund if you request it within 14 days of your original purchase date, and your account hasn't violated our Terms of Service — for example, by using IVD to download content you didn't have the right to.

How to request a refund

Contact us through the Support & Contact page, choose "License & billing" as the topic, and include the email address you purchased with. We'll confirm your order and process the refund from there.

Processing time

Approved refunds are issued to your original payment method and typically appear within 5–7 business days, depending on your bank or card provider.

Monthly Pro subscriptions

You can cancel your Pro subscription at any time to stop future billing. Refunds for the current billing period follow the same 14-day window described above; earlier periods aren't refunded once the service for that period has been used.

Non-refundable cases

Requests made after the 14-day window, purchases showing evidence of Terms of Service violations, and licenses obtained through unauthorized resellers are not eligible for a refund.

Changes to this policy

We'll update the "Last updated" date above whenever this policy changes, and any update applies to purchases made after that date.
