Privacy Policy

How IVD collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: July 6, 2026
This page is a starting-point template, not legal advice. Have a qualified lawyer review it against your actual data practices before publishing.

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains what information IVD (Infinity Video Downloader) collects when you use our desktop application and website, why we collect it, and the choices you have. IVD is built to run mostly on your own device — most of what you download never touches our servers.

Information we collect

When you create an account or purchase a license, we collect your name, email address, and payment details (handled by our payment processor — we never see or store full card numbers). When you use the app, we collect basic usage data such as app version, operating system, and crash reports, so we can keep IVD stable and compatible with your system.

We do not collect or store the videos or audio files you download, nor the links you paste into the app.

How we use your information

We use your information to provide and improve IVD, validate your license, respond to support requests, and send occasional product updates you can opt out of at any time. Usage data helps us identify bugs and prioritize which platforms and formats to support next.

Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share the minimum necessary data with service providers who help us operate — such as our payment processor and email delivery service — and only under contracts that require them to protect it. We may also disclose information if required by law.

Data retention

We keep account information for as long as your account is active, and for a limited period afterward to handle refunds, disputes, or legal obligations. You can request earlier deletion at any time — see "Your rights" below.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information, or to receive a copy of it in a portable format. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details on our Support page.

Children's privacy

IVD is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Changes to this policy

We'll update the "Last updated" date above whenever this policy changes, and let active users know about any significant change through the app or by email.

Contact us

Questions about this policy? Reach out through our Support & Contact page.
